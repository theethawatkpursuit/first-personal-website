My Journey

01

Coding Websites

In high school, I introduced myself to the three main coding languages through HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. A bootcamp helped me grow more comfortable building a website with an online team for the first time. I later met a friend who built web apps and introduced me to React, which made projects more mobile-friendly and interactive.

02

AI Tools

I started using AI tools in my work to avoid drafting every line of code from scratch and to speed up my learning. They also gave me ideas for inspiration when I felt stuck and helped me work through difficult projects during college.

03

Data Scraping

One of my early interests came from data science, where I enjoyed organizing spreadsheet rankings of songs and reshaping the data to show how their results related. AI also helped me build larger formulas and web scrapers so I could gather data for graphs and analysis.

04

Video Game Class

I took a video game class using the Godot engine, and I used ChatGPT extensively to build functioning projects. It was there that I discovered a real passion for AI-assisted development and creating things with a team.

05

Video Editing

Video editing became a side passion as I learned to use editing platforms to turn ideas into visuals. Keyframes and transitions helped me shape motion and style, and I’m now exploring how code can also be used in video editing.

06

Pursuit AI

I attended Pursuit to sharpen the AI-assisted skills I had developed in college and present my learning in a way that would attract future opportunities. I hoped it would guide me toward the experience needed to break into the industry and impress the companies I wanted to work for.
